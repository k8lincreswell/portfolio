when two becomes one

2023, video shot on canon powershot sx730hs

this video was realized in iceland, when, after months of not escaping the city, i was met with empty landscapes, fresh air, and some of the most shockingly beautiful sights i had ever seen. it was a time of pause, reflection, quiet, and recovery. this project was an attempt to convey the somber yet becoming state of mind i was in during this trip.

im writing on my way

2022, song: parfum d'etoiles

originally a class exercise, i ended up taking this video a step further by gracing it with poetry i had written yet never intended to share. this was my first experimentation with words in my work.
